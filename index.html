// FAQ
FREQUENTLY ASKED
Do you offer price matching? ⌄
Yes! We offer price matching on identical products from major authorized retailers. Contact our support team with a link to the lower price and we'll match it within 24 hours.
What is your return policy? ⌄
We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original packaging and unused condition. Once we receive the return, refunds are processed within 2-3 business days.
Do you ship internationally? ⌄
Yes, we ship to 50+ countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Express shipping is available for most major countries.
Can I upgrade the specs on pre-built systems? ⌄
Absolutely! All our pre-built systems are highly customizable. Use our Configure page or contact our build advisors who can tailor any system to your exact requirements and budget.
How long does warranty service take? ⌄
Most warranty repairs are completed within 5-7 business days. For critical components, we offer advance replacement — we ship you a replacement first while you return the defective unit.
Do you offer financing options? ⌄
Yes! We offer 0% interest financing for 12 months on orders over $500, and flexible payment plans for larger purchases. All financing is handled through our secure payment partners.
